The Managing Director’s Remarks at IDB Ghana Launch

Mr Robert Bentil, Managing Director of IDB Ghana in a chat with a cross section of invited guests

The Osu Mantse, His Majesty DF. Nii Okwei Kinka Dowuona VI

The Founder of IDB Ghana Limited, Alhaji Adamu Iddrisu

The Head of Banking Supervision of the Bank of Ghana, Mr. Franklin Belnye

The Chairman of the Board of Directors of IDB Ghana Limited, Dr. K.K. Sarpong

Distinguished Shareholders of IDB Ghana Limited

Distinguished Promoters and Members of the Board of IDB Ghana Limited

Distinguished invited guests

Ladies and Gentlemen

It is my honour to welcome you all to this morning’s function which marks the formal opening of IDB Ghana Limited.

In 2011 the Founder, 2 key promoters and the shareholders had the vision to set up a universal bank, a bank which will provide services which will set it apart from its peers in terms of quality service delivery, competitive pricing and product innovation.

By dint of hard work, determination and focus, this vision has become a reality, one which we are all witnessing today.

With a stated capital of GHS100, 000,000, IDB Ghana Limited is a new entrant with the financial capacity way above the minimum industry requirement. The beauty of IDB Ghana is that it is a wholly owned Ghanaian Bank, which has been set up by Ghanaians to be managed by Ghanaians, with the focus of achieving excellence in world class banking service delivery. This focus is underpinned by our vision which is:

  “To be the Bank of Choice”

   Coupled with the Mission which is:

“To provide an excellent, innovative and world-class banking experience”

Our product offering includes; savings accounts, current accounts, treasury products, international trade finance products to mention a few, all tailor-made for all segments of customers.

At IDB Ghana, Customer satisfaction is our passion at all times.

We can assure our customers that our operations will be supported by a robust IT system which will ensure efficient service delivery and customer satisfaction at all times.

To our regulators we can give the assurance that we have carefully selected the best in terms of experience in the industry, to manage the Bank and to ensure that the customers’ trust is earned at all times. Key members of MY MANAGEMENT TEAM include:

  1. MR. CHARLES OTOO                                DEPUTY MANAGING DIRECTOR
  2. MR. KOFI  ANOKYE OWUSU-DARKO     HEAD, BANKING OPERATIONS
  3. MR. EVANS AMARTEY                                HEAD, IT
  4. MR. E.K. AMINARH                                      HEAD, BUSINESS BANKING
  5. MR. KWAME ANNOH                                  HEAD, CREDIT ADMIN AND MONIT.
  6. MR. BEN DANQUAH                                   HEAD, HR & ADMIN.
  7. MR. DAVID OKYERE NYARKOH                HEAD, FINANCE
  8. MS. ANNITA BEDZRA                                  HEAD LEGAL & COMPANY SECRETARY
  9. MR. THOMPSON DWUMAH                       HEAD,TREASURY
  10. MR. HENRY YARTEY                                   HEAD, INTERNAL AUDIT
  11. MR. NUKU ATTIPOE                                    HEAD, RISK MANAGEMENT & COMPLIANCE

 

Ladies and gentlemen, it is once again my honour and pleasure to welcome you to this all important function.

 

Thank you all.
