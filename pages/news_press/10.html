The Royal Bank Opens More Branches

Robert Bentil-Managing Director

Within six (6) months of its operations The Royal Bank (TRB) has opened Three (3) additional branches in Accra Central, Arena and on the Graphic road in Accra. The opening of these branches brings to five (5) the number of branches after the Osu and Tema branches were launched earlier this year.

The Managing Director of the Bank, Mr. Robert Bentil explained that The Royal Bank will be spreading out to other parts of Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi and Sefwi Asawinso. Mr. Bentil added that the Bank has envisaged in its medium term strategic plan to open 25 branches by 2015, stressing that "Our ultimate objective is to bring the Royal Banking experience to the doorstep of our cherished customers.

Poised to make an impact in the Banking industry in Ghana and beyond, The Royal Bank's focus of banking will be in Agricultural Financing, Oil, Energy and Mining. Other key area of focus will be in Telecommunication, Service and Hospitality and Commerce. In a bid to drive businesses in these sectors of the economy, TRB has invested heavily in a robust IT platform of international standard which will effectively and efficiently assist to deliver excellent customer service to its customers. Mr. Bentil also hinted plans of the introduction of ATM Service in due course, adding that the Bank's robust IT infrastructure will help deliver real time electronic payment service.

Mr. Bentil was of the view that, since the Bank launched its services about some 6 months ago, it has been received very well, stressing that the Bank's Private Banking Service has been well patronized. "We have invested adequately in a banking infrastructure that is dedicated to giving meaning to our tagline - Real Banking. Real Difference". He emphasized.
